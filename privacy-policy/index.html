Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data — simply and transparently.

Last updated: July 18, 2025

Overview

Fuence Podcast ("we," "us," or "our") operates www.fuence.com to provide podcast content, research publications, petition campaigns, and membership-based community access. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU/EEA residents and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents.

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate our services:

  • Contact Form: Names and message content submitted via our contact form (powered by Google Forms). We do not collect or store your email address directly.
  • Petitions: Signatures and related data submitted via petition campaigns hosted by Change.org.
  • Donations and Contributions: Payment information processed securely by third-party platforms (Buy Me a Coffee, Ko-fi) using Stripe as the payment processor. We do not store payment card details.
  • Memberships: Subscription data managed by Patreon and Buy Me a Coffee. We receive only the information those platforms share with us (e.g., membership tier, active status).
  • Cookies: YouTube video embeds may set cookies for analytics or functionality. We do not directly set tracking cookies on this Site.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to:

  • Respond to enquiries submitted via our contact form.
  • Manage petition campaigns through Change.org.
  • Process donations and contributions via third-party platforms.
  • Manage membership subscriptions and provide associated benefits.
  • Maintain community access (e.g., Discord channel authorisation).
  • Comply with legal obligations where required.

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or advertising purposes without your explicit prior consent.

3. Contact Form Data

Our contact form is powered by Google Forms (Google LLC, United States). To submit the form, you are required to sign in with a Google account, which allows Google to share your name with us via its Sign-In API. We do not collect or store your email address.

By checking the "I agree" box in the form, you provide explicit consent to the processing of your data as described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us through the contact form, though withdrawal does not affect data already processed.

A manual verification question is used to prevent spam and does not collect additional personal data. Data submitted via the form is processed by Google LLC and may be stored on servers in the United States. Google complies with GDPR via standard contractual clauses. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for further details.

Form responses are retained only as long as necessary to address your enquiry or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time through the contact form.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share your data only with:

  • Service Providers: Google Forms (USA), YouTube (USA), Stripe (USA), Buy Me a Coffee (USA), Ko-fi (Ireland/USA), Patreon (USA), Change.org (USA), and Discord (USA) process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority, we may disclose data to comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely by our third-party service providers (Google, Stripe, Patreon, Change.org, Discord). We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and you submit data at your own risk.

Data Breach Notification: In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, where required by the GDPR. If the breach is likely to result in a high risk to your rights, we will also notify you directly without undue delay.

6. Cookies

This Site does not set first-party tracking or advertising cookies. YouTube video embeds may set third-party cookies from Google/YouTube for video functionality and analytics. You can manage and block these cookies through your browser settings. For more information, see Google's Cookie Policy.

7. Restricted Content and Members

For authorised members accessing Restricted Content, we may store minimal information necessary to verify and maintain access (e.g., membership tier, access status). This information is not shared with third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the membership platforms. Access records may be retained for the duration of the membership and for a reasonable period thereafter for legal and operational purposes.

8. Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict processing of your data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes.
  • CCPA Rights (California residents): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the contact form. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law (within 30 days under GDPR; within 45 days under CCPA).

11. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately through the contact form and we will promptly delete it.
